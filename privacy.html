Privacy Policy

Effective [TBD — EFFECTIVE DATE] · TACC Pte Ltd · Singapore
In short

We collect what we need to admit you to the register, verify your eligibility, and route deals to you. We don't sell your data. We don't run third-party trackers. We keep KYC five years after our relationship ends and deal records seven years post-close because Singapore law tells us to. You have the rights PDPA and GDPR give you, and the contact below works.

Aurum Prism (the "Platform") is operated by TACC Pte Ltd, a private limited company incorporated in Singapore (UEN [TBD — UEN]), registered office [TBD — REGISTERED ADDRESS, SINGAPORE]. TACC is the data controller for personal data processed through the Platform.

§ 03

Why we collect it · Lawful bases

  • Contract performance. Operating the Platform for you — admission, deal routing, IOI processing, closing — requires processing identity, contact, and transactional data.
  • Legal obligation. KYC, anti-money-laundering screening, sanctions checks, and accredited-investor verification are required under Singapore's Securities and Futures Act and the MAS AML/CFT Notice.
  • Legitimate interest. Audit logging, fraud prevention, dispute defence, and platform security.
  • Consent. Marketing emails outside an active deal relationship — withdrawable at any time without affecting your account.
§ 04

How long we keep it

  • KYC documents and verification records: five (5) years after the end of our relationship — MAS Notice SFA04-N02.
  • Deal participation records, IOIs, subscription docs, audit logs: seven (7) years post-close or post-termination — MAS recordkeeping requirements and Singapore tax/audit law.
  • Marketing consent records: until withdrawn, plus three (3) years for evidentiary purposes.
  • Session cookies: deleted on logout or at TTL expiry (12h operator, 7d advisor, 30d investor).

§ 06

International transfers

Because Vercel and several sub-processors operate from the United States and the European Union, your data will transit and be processed outside Singapore. Where data leaves Singapore, we rely on the recipient's PDPA-comparable protections, the EU Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable, or your contractual consent given at admission.

§ 07

Cookies and similar technology

The Platform sets essential session cookies only — three HttpOnly JWTs (prism_admin, prism_advisor, prism_inst) used to keep you logged in. There are no analytics cookies, no advertising tags, no third-party trackers, no pixels. We do not load Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight, or comparable tools on this site.

§ 08

Your rights

Under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Access. Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification. Have inaccurate data corrected.
  • Erasure. Have your data deleted, subject to our recordkeeping obligations above.
  • Portability (GDPR). Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction and objection (GDPR). Limit or object to certain processing.
  • Withdrawal of consent. Withdraw consent for any processing based on consent, at any time.
  • Complaint. Lodge a complaint with Singapore's Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) or, in the EU, your local supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@theaurumcc.com. We respond within 30 days.

§ 09

Security

Passwords are bcrypt-hashed (cost 12). Session tokens are HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax, Secure in production. Data at rest is encrypted by our hosting and storage providers. The audit log records every login, document view, and stage transition. We notify affected members and the PDPC of qualifying data breaches within the timelines mandated by the PDPA.

§ 10

Changes to this policy

We will post any material change here and notify registered members by email at least 30 days before the change takes effect. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
