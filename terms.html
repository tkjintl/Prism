§ 03

Eligibility

To be admitted and to remain a Member in good standing, you must qualify as one or more of the following at the time of admission and at the time of each transaction:

  • An accredited investor as defined in section 4A of the Singapore Securities and Futures Act (Cap. 289);
  • An institutional investor as defined in the same statute;
  • For US persons or US-targeted offerings, an accredited investor within the meaning of Rule 501(a) and able to participate in offerings made under Rule 506(c) of Regulation D under the US Securities Act of 1933;
  • A registered deal advisor acting in a professional capacity, where applicable.

You represent that the information you provide on admission and on each IOI is accurate, complete, and current. TACC may verify any representation at any time and may suspend or terminate access if eligibility cannot be confirmed.
