How It Works

Three parties.
One pipeline.

Advisors source and submit deals. Prism operators vet and publish. Members and institutions access curated deal flow — all on one platform at prism.theaurumcc.com.

01
Advisors Submit Deals
Prism-approved advisors source deals and submit via the Advisor Deal Room. Each deal goes through Prism's investment committee before being published. Documents staged by deal phase — teaser through audited financials.
02
Prism Vets and Publishes
Partners review, approve, and publish deals to the marketplace. Admitted investors see live deal flow in their marketplace portfolio. Qualified institutional investors access via the marketplace. Partner approval gate controls data room access.
03
Capital Deploys Digitally
Submit IOI, unlock the data room, review watermarked documents. Subscription docs issued in-platform. Wire instructions delivered digitally. Position tracked from deployment through to exit and distribution — no paper, no friction.
