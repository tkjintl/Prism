Investment Overview

Orchard Street Credit Partners II targets senior secured lending opportunities across Southeast Asian mid-market corporates, focusing on asset-backed structures with strong covenant packages and predictable cash flow profiles. The strategy leverages the manager's proprietary origination network built over a decade of regional private credit deployment.

The fund employs a disciplined portfolio construction approach, targeting 18–22 positions across Singapore, Indonesia, and Vietnam, with a maximum single-obligor concentration of 8%. Current pipeline visibility exceeds 3.2x the target raise across identified deal opportunities.

Risk mitigation is central to the strategy — all positions require first-priority security, personal guarantees from operating shareholders, and quarterly covenant testing. The prior vintage returned 15.2% net IRR over its 4-year investment period, significantly outperforming regional benchmarks.
